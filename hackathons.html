Hackathons and Collaborative Prototyping

I participate in hackathons because I enjoy challenging myself in unfamiliar, time-constrained environments. These events push me to understand a problem quickly, contribute to a team, communicate ideas clearly, and turn early concepts into working prototypes.

They have also given me opportunities to work with cross-cultural teams and people from different academic and technical backgrounds. For me, hackathons are not only competitions; they are practical spaces for learning, collaboration, leadership, and building systems that respond to real user needs.
